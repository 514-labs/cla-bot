# Contributor FAQ

## Why is my PR still failing after I signed?
CLA checks are synchronized asynchronously after signing. Wait briefly, then refresh the PR checks page.

## Why do I need to re-sign?
If the org updated its CLA text/hash, older signatures become outdated and you must sign the latest version.

## I signed for one org. Why am I still blocked in another org?
CLA compliance is organization-scoped. Signing for org A does not satisfy org B.

## Where can I see everything I signed?
Use `/contributor` to view your current and historical signatures.

## Can I download older CLA versions I signed?
Yes. From `/contributor`, use the download action for the signature record you want.
